Our Mission

We believe that a clean environment is a fundamental right. EcoPickup bridges the gap between citizens and municipal waste workers to ensure efficient and transparent garbage collection.

Smart Waste Management

Founded on the principles of sustainability and civic responsibility, EcoPickup provides a modern digital infrastructure for waste operations.

From allowing users to quickly report open garbage dumps, to providing sanitation workers with an optimized dashboard for their daily tasks, we use technology to make cities greener.

Community Driven Impact

A city is only as clean as its citizens are involved. We incentivize eco-friendly behavior through a gamified leaderboard system, rewarding those who consistently make the right choices for the environment.

Join thousands of others in the Clean City Mission. Together, we can eradicate open dumping and build a circular economy.
